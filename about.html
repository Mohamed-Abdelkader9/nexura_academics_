Founder of Nexura Academics

Mohamed Abdelkader

My journey in Computer Science began as a curious mind wanting to understand the intricacies of how technology was changing society around us. Over the years I became immersed in AI, Embedded Systems, Cybersecurity, and Web Development while beginning to understand the infinite potential in innovation. Nexura Academics was created out of a passion to allow young minds the opportunity to explore, learn, and develop meaningful solutions to complex issues while providing the encouragement, mentoring, and opportunities that I wished I had available to me in the beginning.

I believe knowledge is most valuable when it is shared, and technology can be a force for good for the advancement of society. Through Nexura Academics, I hope to create a community of inspired learners, innovators, and problem-solvers who are ready to tackle daunting issues of the future. Each project, mentorship, or program is tailored to promote curiosity, develop relevant skills, and cultivate a love of learning.
